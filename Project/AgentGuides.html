Agent Guides

Select which role you would like help with.

Duelists

Entry fraggers focused on aggressive play. They initiate fights and create space for the team.

Sentinels

Defensive experts who lock down areas, provide intel, and protect flanks with traps and heals.

Initiators

Break through enemy defenses with flash, recon, or stun abilities. Great at disrupting setups.

Controllers

Control sightlines with smokes and area denial. Vital for map control and team strategy.
