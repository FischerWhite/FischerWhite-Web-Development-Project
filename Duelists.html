Duelists

Agent Description
Jett An agile entry fragger with high mobility and sharp reflexes.
Abilities:
  • Cloudburst: Short smoke for quick peeks.
  • Updraft: Launch upward for surprise elevation.
  • Tailwind: Dash to escape or reposition.
  • Blade Storm: Deadly throwing knives (Ultimate).
Tips:
  • Use Updraft + Tailwind for vertical flanks.
  • Cloudburst lets you block sightlines when entering a site.
  • Blade Storm is ideal for eco rounds or clutching.

Watch: Jett Beginner Guide (YouTube)
Reyna Capitalizes on kills to fuel her aggressive, self-sustaining play.
Abilities:
  • Leer: Nearsight orb that passes through walls.
  • Devour: Heals you after getting a kill.
  • Dismiss: Turn intangible and reposition.
  • Empress: Supercharged state with healing and faster fire (Ultimate).
Tips:
  • Use Leer before entering fights to reduce enemy visibility.
  • Choose Devour or Dismiss after each kill based on health/safety.
