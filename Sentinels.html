Sentinels

Agent Description
Sage A supportive Sentinel who excels at healing and area control.
Abilities:
  • Barrier Orb: Deploys a solid wall that can be rotated before placement.
  • Slow Orb: Casts a slowing field that reduces enemy movement speed.
  • Healing Orb: Heals an ally or herself over time.
  • Resurrection (Ultimate): Revives a dead teammate with full health.
Tips:
  • Use Barrier Orb to block enemy paths or boost to unexpected angles.
  • Slow Orb is effective for delaying enemy pushes or securing retreats.
  • Prioritize healing allies during engagements to maintain team strength.
  • Resurrection can turn the tide of a round; use it strategically.

Cypher A surveillance expert who gathers intelligence and controls the battlefield.
Abilities:
  • Trapwire: Deploys a tripwire between two walls, revealing and dazing enemies who cross it.
  • Cyber Cage: Tosses a remote-activated trap that slows enemies who pass through it.
  • Spycam: Places a remote camera that can fire a tracking dart at enemies.
  • Neural Theft (Ultimate): Reveals the location of all living enemies by extracting information from a dead enemy.
Tips:
  • Place Trapwires in common paths to gather intel and delay enemy advances.
  • Use Cyber Cages in conjunction with Trapwires for maximum disruption.
  • Spycam provides valuable information; position it to cover key areas.
  • Neural Theft is best used after securing a kill in a safe area.
