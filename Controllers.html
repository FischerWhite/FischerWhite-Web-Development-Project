Controllers

Agent Description
Brimstone A tactical Controller with powerful smokes and fire-based utility.
Abilities:
  • Stim Beacon: Deploys a beacon that grants RapidFire to nearby allies.
  • Incendiary: Grenade that creates a damaging fire zone.
  • Sky Smoke: Deploy up to 3 long-lasting smokes anywhere on the map.
  • Orbital Strike (Ultimate): Laser beam that deals high damage over time in an area.
Tips:
  • Use Sky Smoke to block off sightlines during site takes or defuses.
  • Orbital Strike is great for post-plant pressure or denying defuses.
  • Stim Beacon can boost teammates during retakes or rushes.

Watch: Brimstone Guide (YouTube)
Viper A poisonous Controller who uses chemical devices to control the battlefield.
Abilities:
  • Snake Bite: Damaging acid that also applies vulnerability.
  • Poison Cloud: A gas emitter that creates a toxic smoke when activated.
  • Toxic Screen: A long gas wall that blocks vision and damages enemies.
  • Viper's Pit (Ultimate): Massive cloud that obscures vision and lowers enemy health.
Tips:
  • Combo Poison Cloud and Snake Bite to trap enemies in vulnerable zones.
