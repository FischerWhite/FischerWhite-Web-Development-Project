Ascent Map Strategies

Ascent features a large mid and strong defensive setups. Use these pro-level strategies to dominate this map.

🧠 General Tips

  • Control Mid — it unlocks access to both A and B sites.
  • Use smokes to block Defender sightlines from Market and Catwalk.
  • Agents like Sova, Killjoy, and Omen perform well on Ascent.

🎯 Attack Strategies

  • Mid to B Split: Take Mid Control → Smoke Market → Push through B Main and Mid.
  • A Site Rush: Use Initiator utility (like KAY/O or Breach) to clear Generator and Dice.

🛡️ Defense Setups

  • B Site: Hold from Logs or Switch with Killjoy turret watching Lane.
  • A Site: Play off angles from Heaven or Door, smoke A Main early.
