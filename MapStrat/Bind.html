Bind Map Strategies

Bind features two teleporters and no mid-lane, encouraging creative site control and fast rotates. Use these pro-level tips to get ahead.

🧠 General Tips

  • Control Showers and Hookah to dominate both sites.
  • Use teleporters to fake rotations or escape.
  • Great agents: Raze, Viper, Cypher, and Skye.

🎯 Attack Strategies

  • Split Push: Attack both A and B to confuse defenders.
  • Fake Rotate: Teleport after using utility on one site to bait rotations.
  • Clear Common Angles: Smoke CT, Flash Elbow, and check Cubby & Lamps.

🛡️ Defense Setups

  • Early Info: Use Sova dart or Chamber trap to scout for rushes.
  • Hold U-Hall and Elbow: These are key control points for each site.
  • Play for Retake: Use teleporters to reposition fast if needed.
