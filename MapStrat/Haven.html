Haven Map Strategies

Haven is the only map in Valorant with three bomb sites. It rewards strong rotations, site holds, and map control. Here’s how to master it.

🧠 General Tips

  • Three sites means defenders need to spread thin — rotate smart.
  • Control Mid (Garage and Window) to create pressure and options.
  • Agents like Killjoy, Omen, Sova, and Breach excel here.

🎯 Attack Strategies

  • Fast C Rush: Use smokes and flashes to enter through Garage and C Long.
  • Mid Control: Take Garage and Window to pressure B or split to A.
  • Fake & Rotate: Early noise at A, rotate through Spawn or Mid to C.

🛡️ Defense Setups

  • A Site: Play from Heaven or behind Default; breach and smokes are common.
  • Garage: Use Cypher trip or Killjoy utility to delay pushes.
  • Fast Rotations: Prioritize fast rotate agents like Omen and Skye.
