Split Map Strategies

Split is a vertically-stacked map with tight chokepoints and hard-to-clear angles. Controlling Mid is key to breaking through strong defenses.

🧠 General Tips

  • Always contest Mid — whoever controls it controls the game flow.
  • Use vertical utility (Raze, Omen, Viper walls) to pressure Heaven positions.
  • Recommended agents: Sage, Raze, Breach, Viper, Killjoy.

🎯 Attack Strategies

  • Mid to B: Take Mid → Smoke Heaven → Split from Mail and B Main.
  • A Heaven Control: Flash or wall Heaven → drop into site from Ramp.
  • B Rush: Fast utility through Garage and B Main to overwhelm defenders.

🛡️ Defense Setups

  • Mid Defense: Sage wall Mid Bottom or peek with an Operator from Vents.
  • A Site: Play from Heaven and Ramp with crossfire setups.
  • B Site: Anchor with utility behind Pillar or Default.
